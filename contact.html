Frequently Asked Questions

The 30-day trading strategy is a systematic approach to growing your investment by a set percentage each day while protecting your capital through savings. The plan calculates your expected profits and savings based on your initial investment and chosen daily percentage.

Yes, you can update your initial amount and daily percentage at any time from your profile page. However, changing these values will reset your trading plan.

Savings are calculated as 50% of your daily profit minus any lost money for that day. This helps protect your capital by setting aside a portion of your profits.

If you mark a day as not achieved, that day's profit won't be added to your balance, and the subsequent days will be recalculated based on your current balance.
